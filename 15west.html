Room 1029A

This is an actually existing room in Rhode Island School of Design, 15 Westminster St Providence. The website is a prototype of the space where students, especially freshmen, can access and grab some information about a particular room. Ideally, the website will be designed with further detailed conditions, and has every room in 15 West.

The model was initially 3D rendered with Rhino, under Adam Smith's workshop 2015.
